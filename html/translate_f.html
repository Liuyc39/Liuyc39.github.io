1 Cited Introduction

Rock has high strength and small deformation , and is the first choice natural foundation in construction engineering . However, at the same time because it has a discontinuous rock heterogeneity and anisotropy, etc. , so that theoretically determined ROCK bearing capacity study far less computing theoretical bearing capacity of foundation soil as perfect. Especially for the determination of the bearing capacity of the rock slope near the slope, there is not much research on classical theoretical solutions at home and abroad . Regarding the calculation method of bearing capacity of rock foundation , the most typical classical calculation method is the Bell solution proposed in 1915 [1] . The B ell solution is suitable for the calculation of the bearing capacity of homogeneous rock masses including intact and weak fractures, so it is widely used to determine the bearing capacity of broken rock masses. The B ell solution indicates the existence of rock foundation bearing capacity and rock mass structural plane Close relationship, at the same time, the most common shear failure mode of rock is proposed , and the theoretical calculation formula of the ultimate bearing capacity of rock foundation is determined by the limit equilibrium analysis method .

Later, with the development of plastic mechanics, the use of limit analysis method in rock and soil has made great progress. Because the limit analysis method is used to analyze the bearing capacity of the foundation, it is not necessary to accurately determine the stress distribution inside each rigid block in the destruction mechanism. It is suitable for bearing capacity and stability analysis of complex geotechnical engineering structures. In the mid-twentieth century , D C Drucker and W Pra G ER [2] for soil build static force field and velocity field derived limit theorem and the theorem of the lower limit, the establishment of a complete theory of limit analysis. American scholar Ch EN [3] summary of existing research results , the system elaborated the principle and limit analysis in geotechnical body and a use some kind of rock material, so limit analysis in geotechnical study has been considerable in progress. Collins et [5] proposed in the study had a double slope failure slider slope failure mode , the failure mode is a computationally simple ; Sa RAN et [5] for sloping Pro-yl consider bilateral sliding failure, using C - M failure criterion, but the soil parameters on both sides of the foundation change, and the limit equilibrium method and the limit analysis method are used to obtain the analytical solution of the problem ; Wang Hongyu et al [6] based on Sokolovsky's static theory of loose media and Prandt l weightless foundation slip network constructs a symmetrical failure mode of the slope foundation, and an asymmetric failure mode of the slope foundation soil is constructed by FLAC-3D ; Yang Feng [8] considers the asymmetry of slope foundation failure and passes the upper limit The ultimate bearing capacity was obtained by analysis, but the influence of slope distance on the ultimate bearing capacity was not considered; Wu Chao et al. [9] For the weightless foundation, the critical slip mode was solved by the mini-genetic algorithm based on non-uniform mutation, and the diagonal bar solution upper bearing capacity of foundation points verification calculation formula N gamma] and Vesic coefficients solution of closest ; Weidong et [10] constructed in consideration of the case has sloping strip foundation Pro asymmetry of failure characteristics side pull failure mode and bilateral sliding failure mode, the motor allows to build velocity field derived both cases Pro slope carrying capacity was shaped foundation formula , then another for the failure mode of the multi-slider, calculated using nonlinear strength criterion slopes foundation ultimate bearing capacity, the study of the rock foundation bearing capacity has reference ; Xiao [13 is] yl obtain the ultimate bearing capacity of the foundation soil slope to the strip movement limit theorem boundary analysis, by varying the internal friction angle, slope Pro Distance, slope angle and other factors that affect the ultimate bearing capacity .

Given the current calculation classic rock foundation engineering Pro Capacity slope method are imperfect, herein for breaking rock slope Pro-yl dual slider failure modes , in order of Be LL solution prototype build a temporary slope yl shear failure mode, using The C- M strength criterion establishes the velocity field allowed by maneuvering, deduces the expression form of foundation bearing capacity and calculates and analyzes it in combination with actual engineering cases , with a view to exploring a convenient calculation method suitable for the calculation of bearing capacity of rock foundation on slope .

Sandaozhuang of large open pit mine is open pit, the lowest open mining design elevation + 1072m , mining depth final 48 . 6 m , the final design of the plate slope angle approximately is . 5 0 [deg.] , Typical of a high and steep slope [16] . The mine on the plate side slope of a hill building has a viewing platform architecture , is a vital building (class Ⅰ), bear propaganda window function mine for enterprise products exhibition, exhibition of mining technology, as well as their superiors, peers who visit , Of great significance to mines and enterprises. In recent years, observation deck minimum distance to the top edge of the base edge of the slope deficiencies 4M , below slope occurs a local landslide , base from sliding groove about the edge . 1 3.5 of m .
